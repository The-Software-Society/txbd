A First-of-Its-Kind Initiative

The First Comprehensive State-Sponsored
Precious Metals Program in the Nation

Established to help promote awareness of the Texas Bullion Depository®, a state agency charged with providing a superior way for investors to protect their precious metals assets, these products represent a historic milestone. Created to advance the Depository’s mission while honoring Texas’ proud heritage, the series reflects the values that have long defined the Lone Star State: independence, strength, resilience, and enduring prosperity.

A Living Series

From Inaugural Release to a Perennial Legacy

2025

The Inaugural Coin Release

The series debuted with a highly limited inaugural release featuring both gold and silver commemorative coins, alongside the introduction of the Modern Redbacks. The obverse presented a sculpted relief map of Texas, anchored by the iconic lone star, establishing the visual and symbolic foundation for what would follow.

Learn more about the 2025 Lone Star
2026

An Iconic New Design

The state commissioned a new perennial design from famed coin designer Joel Iskowitz. The Lone Star captures the enduring spirit of Texas through a striking depiction of Lady Liberty seated beside the Texas State Capitol, a powerful symbol of strength, governance, and Texas independence.

More about the 2026 design
Ahead

Looking Ahead

Envisioned as a long-term initiative, future releases are expected to introduce new designs, each highlighting a distinct chapter of Texas history, culture, people, and landmarks, telling the story of Texas through beautifully crafted precious metal products.

Designed by a Legend

The Final Works of Joel Iskowitz

The 2026 Lone Star designs represent one of the final artistic works of internationally acclaimed master medallic artist Joel Iskowitz, whose work for the U.S. Mint includes the Arizona and District of Columbia quarters and the reverse of the 2009 Lincoln Bicentennial cent.
