State of Texas Commemorative Series

Where to Buy Contact Us

The State of Texas Commemorative Series is a groundbreaking, state-authorized initiative that offers Texans and collectors worldwide the opportunity to own official precious metal products issued by the State of Texas.

A First-of-Its-Kind Initiative in Precious Metals

The Texas Bullion Depository

Established to help promote awareness of the Texas Bullion Depository®—a state agency charged with providing a superior way for investors in Texas and from around the nation and world to protect their precious metals assets—these products represent a historic milestone as the first comprehensive state-sponsored precious metals initiative of its kind in the nation, offering gold and silver coins, precious metals bills, and secure storage options.

Created to advance the Depository’s mission while honoring Texas’ proud heritage, the series reflects the values that have long defined the Lone Star State: independence, strength, resilience, and enduring prosperity. Through expertly crafted gold and silver products, the State of Texas Commemorative Series brings together tangible value, exceptional craftsmanship, and the spirit of Texas.

State of Texas Lone Star Coins

A New Chapter in Texas Independence

As Texans, we are proud of our independent spirit, rooted in the Texas Revolution and the state’s founding as a sovereign republic. The State of Texas Commemorative Series continues that tradition by offering beautifully crafted gold and silver products that reflect both financial strength and state pride.

Similar to other well-known bullion products, State of Texas Lone Star Commemorative Coins are produced with exceptional purity and craftsmanship—designed as collectible works of art while also meeting the high standards of investment-grade bullion.

Commemorative Product Series Overview

The series includes two complementary product lines:

01

Official State of Texas Lone Star Commemorative Coins

Precision-crafted gold and silver commemorative coins featuring iconic Texas imagery and symbolism. While not legal tender, these official State of Texas products are designed to the highest standards and are an IRA-eligible investment.

02

Official State of Texas Commemorative Modern Redbacks

Innovative gold bills that combine historical inspiration with modern design, crafted to celebrate timeless wealth preservation in a unique and visually striking form.

While these products are not legal tender, they are official State of Texas products, positioned at the intersection of collectability, history, and tangible value based on their high purity and precious metals content.

Inaugural Release

The series debuted with a limited inaugural release in 2025, featuring both gold and silver commemorative coins, as well as the introduction of the Modern Redbacks. Select inaugural commemorative coins were issued in limited, high-grade formats, further enhancing their rarity and appeal to collectors.

As the first release in the series, the 2025 State of Texas Lone Stars established the visual and symbolic foundation for what is to come.

2025 Design, Iconography & Symbolism

The inaugural obverse design presents a sculpted relief map of Texas, highlighting the state’s diverse landscape from the mountains of West Texas to the Gulf Coast plains and bays. Encircling the design is the inscription “TEXAS BULLION DEPOSITORY 1 OZ .9999 FINE GOLD,” anchored by the iconic lone star. A subtle TXBD privy mark, layered over the historic “Come and Take It” cannon-and-star emblem, offers a quiet tribute to Texas’ spirit of independence and revolutionary heritage. The reverse design, shared across the series, features the Official Coat of Arms of Texas framed by live oak and olive branches, along with the inscriptions “THE STATE OF TEXAS” and the state motto, “FRIENDSHIP.”

Looking Ahead

The State of Texas Commemorative Series is envisioned as a long-term initiative to promote awareness of the Depository and its services with the hope that the series will continue to grow and evolve in the years ahead. Future releases are expected to introduce new designs, each highlighting a distinct chapter of Texas history, culture, people, landmarks, and enduring traditions.

By introducing new themes and artwork over time, the series aims to create an ongoing series that tells the story of Texas through beautifully crafted precious metal products. Each release will offer collectors and Texans alike the opportunity to commemorate the spirit and legacy of the Lone Star State in a tangible and enduring form.

More than a precious metals product series, this initiative represents a lasting tribute to Texas heritage—uniting history, craftsmanship, and tangible value in a way that is both meaningful and uniquely Texan.
