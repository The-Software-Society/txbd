A Design Rooted in Texas Heritage

The 2026 1 oz Gold State of Texas Lone Star features a design that honors both Texas history and identity.

The obverse presents a graceful, stylized depiction of Lady Liberty seated beside the Texas State Capitol. With elegant detail, Lady Liberty extends an arm outward, holding the Lone Star above the Capitol dome, symbolizing guidance, aspiration, and the enduring ideals of freedom. The architectural rendering of the Capitol anchors the design in Texas heritage, while the placement of the year ‘2026’ below provides a balanced and refined finish.

The reverse displays the official Coat of Arms of Texas, a hallmark of the series. Centered by the Lone Star, symbolizing Texas independence, the design is encircled by live oak and olive branches, representing strength, resilience, peace, and prosperity. The inscriptions “THE STATE OF TEXAS” and “FRIENDSHIP” reinforce the commemorative coin’s connection to Texas heritage.

What the Branches Represent on the Texas Seal

The branches on the reverse are not merely decorative; they carry deep symbolic meaning tied to Texas identity and values. The olive branch on the left represents peace, reflecting a desire for harmony and diplomacy. The live oak branch on the right represents strength, endurance, and resilience, symbolizing the toughness of the Texas spirit. Together, they convey that Texas stands for both strength and peace: resilient in defense, yet grounded in unity and stability.

About the Series

This commemorative series, authorized by the State of Texas and produced under the Texas Bullion Depository®, combines exceptional design, .9999 fine gold purity, and enduring Texan symbolism. It stands as a powerful tribute to the heritage, independence, and lasting legacy of the Lone Star State.

Purity

State of Texas Lone Stars are minted from precious metals of the highest purity, comparable to sovereign issues from the U.S. Mint, Royal Canadian Mint, and The Royal Mint. Gold State of Texas Coins are 24k, containing 99.99% pure gold.

Available in specialized tamper-evident Certi-Lock® packaging, each 2026 1 oz Gold State of Texas Lone Star is securely encapsulated to help protect its condition and verify authenticity through advanced digital technology. Designed for added confidence and peace of mind, this premium packaging enhances both the security and collectability of the coin. Learn more ›

While obverse designs may change, the reverse of every Texas commemorative coin remains constant, the defining hallmark of the series. At its center is the Official Coat of Arms of Texas, framed by the live oak and olive branches and the state motto, “FRIENDSHIP,” establishing continuity across all future releases.

Meet the Designer

Joel Iskowitz

Master Medallic Artist · U.S. Mint Designer
